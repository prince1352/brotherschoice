Restaurants & Grocery Stores

Serving Edmonton and Western Canada with Quality Products Since 2015

Grocery Store Supplier

Brothers Choice Foods proudly supplies independent grocers, community stores, and national chains with high-quality grocery products tailored to South Asian and multicultural markets. From essential pantry staples to hard-to-find specialty items, we help grocery retailers meet the diverse needs of their customers with reliable delivery and exceptional service.

Specialty South Asian Products

Our retail clients rely on us to supply an extensive range of high-demand South Asian products. From basmati rice and ghee to authentic spices, snacks, and beverages, we source directly from trusted global importers and regional producers. Each product is carefully selected to meet the tastes and preferences of our multicultural customers.

Staple Grocery Products

We’re proud to be a trusted supplier of everyday staples to independent grocers. From lentils, flour, paneer, dahi and ready-to-eat goods, we work with reputable manufacturers to deliver consistently fresh and competitively priced essentials for grocery shelves across Western Canada.

Restaurant Supplier

As a trusted wholesale partner, Brothers Choice Foods works closely with restaurants across Western Canada to supply fresh produce, basa fillets, authentic spices, cooking oils, and specialty ingredients. Whether you're a family-run eatery or a large-scale kitchen, our mission is to provide the high-quality ingredients and dependable service you need to craft memorable dishes.

Fresh Produce

We supply restaurants with fresh, seasonal produce sourced from local farms and trusted national vendors. Our network ensures timely delivery of top-quality vegetables, herbs, and fruits to keep your kitchen stocked and your dishes fresh.

Frozen Foods

We supply a dependable range of frozen vegetables, fish, desserts, and traditional South Asian sweets, perfectly suited for restaurants and commercial kitchens. Our frozen products are carefully sourced and preserved to maintain quality, flavour, and freshness, helping you save prep time without compromising on taste.

Dairy Products

From paneer and yogurt to butter and milk powders, we supply restaurants with essential dairy ingredients for traditional and modern recipes. All products are refrigerated and delivered promptly to preserve freshness.

Dry Goods & Grains

Our dry goods selection includes premium basmati rice, atta, pulses, lentils, and flours used in South Asian and global cuisine. Trusted by chefs for their consistency, shelf life, and authentic quality.
