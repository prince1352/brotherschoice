Meet the Brothers Choice Team

Brothers Choice Foods was founded by four brothers who immigrated to Canada with a shared vision of building something lasting together. Through their hard work, strong family values, and deep understanding of customer needs, they’ve shaped over 25 years of experience in the South Asian grocery business—prioritizing both quality products and exceptional service.

Since launching the Brothers Choice Foods brand, the company has grown from a small local venture into a Western Canada-wide distribution network. Today, we proudly supply independent grocers, national retailers, and food service providers with premium South Asian and ethnic grocery items, specialty imports, and ingredients trusted by chefs and communities alike.
