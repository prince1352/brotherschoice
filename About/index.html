Meet The Brothers Choice Team

Brothers Choice Foods was founded by the Khaira brothers, four siblings who immigrated to Canada with a shared vision of building something lasting together. Their journey began with the launch of All India Supermarket, a locally owned grocery store that quickly became a cornerstone of the South Asian community in Edmonton. Through hard work, family values, and a deep understanding of customer needs, the brothers turned a single retail location into a trusted name, not just for grocery products, but for service and reliability.

Building on their retail success, the Khaira brothers expanded into wholesale distribution, launching the Brothers Choice Foods brand. What began as a small-scale operation has since grown into a Western Canada-wide distribution network, offering an ever-growing range of South Asian and ethnic food products. Today, Brothers Choice proudly supplies independent grocers, national retailers, and food service providers with high-quality ingredients, specialty imports, and a commitment to excellence rooted in family tradition.
