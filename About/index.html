Meet The Brothers Choice Team

Brothers Choice Foods was founded by four brothers, who immigrated to Canada with a shared vision of building something lasting together. Through their hard work, family values, and a deep understanding of customer needs, the brothers have been in the south asian grocery business for over 25 years, not just for grocery products, but for service and reliability.

After launching launching the Brothers Choice Foods brand, the small-scale operation has grown into a Western Canada-wide distribution network, offering an ever-growing range of South Asian and ethnic food products. Today, Brothers Choice proudly supplies independent grocers, national retailers, and food service providers with high-quality ingredients, specialty imports, and a commitment to excellence rooted in family tradition.
